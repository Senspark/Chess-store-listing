Chess kuyinto umdlalo webhodi udlalwa emkhatsini abadlali ababili. It is wadlala ebhodini chess, okuyinto ibhodi square-oxube okubi nokuhle 64 tikwele ihlelwe grid ayisishiyagalombili-by-ayisishiyagalombili.
Ekuqaleni the game, umdlali ngamunye ilawula eyishumi nesithupha izingcezu: 1 inkosi, 1 indlovukazi, ababhishobhi 2, 2 knights, 2 Ehlasela no 8 pawns. Into the game iwukuba checkmate inkosi abaphikisi sika, lapho inkosi iyahlaselwa ngokushesha (e 'hlola') futhi ayikho indlela ukususa noma uvikeleke ekuhlaselweni sendleleni olandelayo.
